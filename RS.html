BORDER REGIMENT
"LOYAL AND ALERT"
Based on the operational concepts that support the National Border Management Policy, the Brigade Border is in charge of controlling border security from any form of intrusion, infiltration, smuggling and criminal activity. It will also work with other local Public Authorities to promote the cooperation of socio-economic development in the border areas.
