ROYAL SERVICE CORPS
"SERVE TO EVERYONE"
Commissioned as an Army logistics team as workshops and transport companies. Each brigade has one logistic company and a brigade workshop. While in every division one logistic with four companies in it. These include such a logistic company, air transport companies, supply companies and headquarters companies. At the battalion level it was headed by Lieutenant Colonel. And every company is headed by  Major or Captain Officer

Corps Role

  1. LOGISTIC - Responsible to manage all military logistic by using military truck, jeep or airplane. Normally, there is one battalion of Royal Service Corps in every brigade.
  2. FOOD SUPPLY - incharge all type of food suply starting from dry food, wet food and combat ration to all military personal.
  3. CATERING - This unit will consist military chef to serve food for soilders or any national big ceremony thats involve the highest rank minister or king.
  4. FIREFIGHTER-To give firefighter support in millitary and also support local firefighter if needed.
  5. FUEL SUPPLY-will incharge all petrol station for millitary assest in every military camp.
