ROYAL SIGNALS REGIMENT
"Swift and Sure"
Royal Signals Regiment is a combat support unit in the Malaysian Army. Royal Signals Regiment is responsible for procuring and maintaining channels of communication to headquarters and tactical elements of the Malaysian Army. Royal Signals Regiment also responsible for managing all aspects of the implementation of electronic warfare and early warning system for the Malaysian Army.
