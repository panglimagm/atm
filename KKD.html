KOR KESIHATAN DIRAJA
"PREFERRED SERVICE"

Royal Medical Corps provides training for Army medics and other specialists. It runs the Armed Forces hospitals and provides the batllefield mobile hospitals.The units also provided relief MALMEDTIMs (Malaysian Medical Teams) to Pakistan and Indonesia

TYPES OF MEDICAL INFANTRY 

PARATROOPERS MEDICAL SQUADRON

Paratroopers medical squadron or better know as PARA VIPER is a special medical team that can be deployed from air,sea and land to conduct medical service in battlefield, Build combat hospitals 10km from battelfield  that can hold up to 30 patients in one time.

.

GENERAL MEDICAL

Providing health service to the military and their families. Services consists of military hospitals, medicines, surgery and dentistry. 

.

COMBAT MEDIC

Consists of infantry members who receives medical training and as a first medical response to treat the wounded members in the battlefield and bring out to the safe zone.
