The Royal Armor Corps has 5 regiments are equipt with armored personnel carriers (APCs), infantry combat vehicles (IFVs), armored combat vehicles (ACVs) and also with the lightweight Alvis Scorpion. The armored regiment has 3 combat squadron, 1 headquarters squadron, and 1 support squadron. The Armor regiment is usually ruled by the rank of Lieutenant Colonel.
