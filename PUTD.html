ARMY AIR FORCE
The Army Air Force or PUTD is a corpse in the Malaysian Army who is in charge of providing air aid in battlefields to Army units. This team is most recently established in the Malaysian Army. At present, the PUTD is equipped with helicopters in the role of liaison or in the role of observation in the field of observation. The Malaysian Army plans to complete the PUTD functions by equipping a combat helicopter in future.
