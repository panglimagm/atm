ROYAL INTELLIGENCE CORPS
"SMART AND SWIFT"
Royal Intelligence Corps  is a Malaysian intelligence agency specialising in intelligence gathering, intelligence analysis, psychological warfare (i.e., psy-ops), surveillance, espionage, reconnaissance and counter-intelligence operations. It also manages the propaganda and counter-propaganda effort of the Malaysian Army.

INTELLIGENCE BRANCH

  1. Defense Intelligence Staff Division (BSPP) - consists of various levels of intelligence operators or services serving in this unit
  2. 92 Special Branch Armed Forces (ATCK) - to carry out intelligence operations inside or outside the country with infiltration into the organization and government as well as specialized intelligence operations.
  3. 91 Special Combat Intelligence Regiment (RKPT) - Consist of intelligence members and special forces who move in enemy territory using special reconnaissance or imitation methods to find and collect information to military commanders. Implement counter-intelligence and capture the enemy spy.
  4. Intelligence Branch-  collect intelligence data and spy on long distance using cyber systems, special tracking methods, radar, and aerial reconnaissance.
