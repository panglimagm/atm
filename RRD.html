ROYAL RANGER REGIMENT
"as long as we live, we will fight"
The Royal Ranger Regiment is one of the Infantry Corps established on March 16, 1963. The RRD contains 9 battalions in which a battalion is a battalion, a battalion as a mechanical battalion, and the rest is a standard infantry battalion. It is the second largest regiment in the Army. This regiment is a unique regiment in which all the races and religions are composed. In addition to Malay, Chinese, Indian and Sikh descent, the Royal Ranger Regiment also comprises Bumiputera members from Sabah and Sarawak and the Orang Asli and there are also Gurkha (who are Malaysian citizens, rather than permanent residents) who also serve in this regiment.

TYPE OF INFANTRY BATTALION

INFANTRY STANDARD BATTALION
Infantry standard battalion will enter the battlefield by walking or using transport. They will be equipped with a rifle and grouped according to battles Section, Platoon, Company, and Battalion. An infantry member armed with a combat rifle will be assisted by members of aid arm groups such as LMG, GPMG, Mortar and anti-tank rocket.

PARATROOPERS
Paratroopers are one of the army elite units. They are highly skilled in accordance with its status as a Rapid Deployment Force. The paratroopers are vigorously trained for specialized combat operations and rapid deployment. The 10 Para can be deployed from land, air, and sea. They also can be parachuted from an airplane with some light tank. They also will be trained with a different type of skills such as:-
-Close Quarters Combat (CQC)
-HALO/HAHO
-Sniper
-Demolition expert
-Pathfinder

MECHANIZE
Mechanize Infantry Battalion operations are using armored vehicles to bring the infantry at the battlefield. The mechanize battalion moves as part of a combined arms group together with an armored regiment to seeks, approaches and destroys the enemy . When fighting, mechanize infantry will come out of their armored vehicles and fight like a standard infantry. Armored vehicles are using weapons equipment such as machine guns and a 20mm cannon to provide support.
